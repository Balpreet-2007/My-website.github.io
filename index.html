The History of Sikhism

Sikhism

Sikhi more commonly known as Sikhism, is a religion originating in Punjab, India. The religion was found by the first Guru of Sikhism, Guru Nanak Dev Ji in the late 15th century. Sikhism is a monethestic religion.

Facts

Symbols of Sikhism

  • Kesh - Uncut hair
  • Kara - Steel bracelet
  • Kirpan - Small sword

About Sikhism
  1. There are ten human Gurus and the eleventh Guru is the religious text of Sikhism
  2. Gurudwaras are the place where Sikhs worship
  3. Sikhs serve langar which are meals given to anyone at anytime for free
  4. Sikhs wear paggs aka turbans to cover their uncut hair
  5. The official language of Sikhs is Gurmukhi
